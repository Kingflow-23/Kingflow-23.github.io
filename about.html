My Journey

My strong interest in mathematics and computer science naturally led me to the dynamic field of Artificial Intelligence (AI). I am building a solid foundation in both the theoretical and practical aspects of AI, data science, and programming, with the goal of combining technical expertise and creativity to solve real-world problems.

Throughout my studies, as well as in my professional experiences, I have worked on a variety of projects in roles such as Data Scientist, Data Engineer, and Generative AI Engineer. These experiences have given me a broad perspective on the AI ecosystem and allowed me to explore the many ways one can contribute meaningfully to this field.

Driven by curiosity and a commitment to continuous learning, I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to collaborate on innovative AI projects. If you’d like to exchange ideas, share perspectives, or explore potential collaborations, let’s connect ✨
